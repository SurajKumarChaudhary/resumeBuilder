What makes us unique?

Continue without login

We know you don't want to create accounts unnecessarily and we respect your privacy. That's why we are not forcing you to create an account in order to use our service. But creating an account will surely help you in future to easily make changes in your resume. You can login into your account and easily add or delete details wherever required.This can save  a lot of your precious time.





Anonymous communication

Most of the time people don’t communicate because they are not confident with their question or answer. That's why we have a unique feature called Anonymous communication. While asking a question , you can select the Anonymous option. By enabling this you can hide your identity. But this feature can be misused by users for inappropriate / offensive messaging. To avoid that an admin would be always in control who can see details if required.
